Expect Something New

In May 2011, a new, free, open source CMS
will be released. We call it structr.

What is structr?

And why should you use it?

structr is a free, open-source CMS, written in Java, based on the fantastic NoSQL graph database Neo4j. [read more]

By design, structr is modular, distributed and easy to use.

Use it for your personal or corporate website, but also if you are curious about a new way creating interactive websites.

History and Status

The current version is 0.2 (non-public).

structr is not yet released to the public. A first public beta release (probably v0.3) of structr is scheduled for May 2011.

Development of structr started in May 2010.
