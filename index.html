About

structr is a free, open-source CMS, written in Java, based on the fantastic NoSQL graph database Neo4j.

Development of structr began in May 2010. While it is subject to constant further development, the current version is 0.2 (status of 11-MAR-2011). The UI is made with Apache Click, a great JEE web application framework.

By design, structr is modular, distributed and easy to use.

Status

structr is not yet released to the public. A first public beta release (0.3) of structr is scheduled for May 2011.
