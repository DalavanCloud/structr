What is structr?

Why should I use it?

structr is a free, open-source CMS, written in Java, based on the fantastic NoSQL graph database Neo4j.

By design, structr is modular, distributed and easy to use.

History and Status

structr is not yet released to the public. A first public beta release (probably v0.3) of structr is scheduled for May 2011.

Development of structr began in May 2010.

While it is subject to constant further development, the current version is 0.2 (status of 11-MAR-2011).
